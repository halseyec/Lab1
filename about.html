EMMELINE SMITH

I consider myself a connoisseur of all things. I find a lot of value and appreciation in the seemingly insignificant or the commonly overlooked media in our lives. The purpose of this website is to share some insight that I have on these things, and hopefully give them the spotlight they deserve.

I feel like as a college student, other than school, entertainment and food is my only form of solace and escape from the relentless pressures of academia. I feel as though these photos capture something that everyone in my position will be able to relate to, and hopefully be intrigued.


Thank you for visiting my website!
